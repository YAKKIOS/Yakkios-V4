About

I (Jack Johnson) am a Lead Product Designer at Oak Engage based in Newcastle Upon Tyne. Specializing in functionality and creating beautiful interfaces. I have over 7 years experience working in the design field, including agency and in-house.

I believe in creating human-centric products through strategic foundations and collaborative design. Friendly, transparent and communicative I strive to create experiences that delight users and create tangible business value. From ideation to concept realisation, I apply my capabilities to help shape those experiences. I have worked with a wide range of clients both through agency and in house building relationships that last. I am in the process of writing up full case studies for digital projects both past and current, thanks for your patience x
